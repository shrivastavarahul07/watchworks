OUR STORY SINCE 2020

Started in the month of April 2020 during the Covid-19 Lockdown in order to directly fulfill the requirements of the customer when all the retail outlets were closed. This period founded the Swiss Watch Company website offering Rolex, Patek Phillips, Bvlgari, Omega, Cartier, Brietling and Franck Muller watches at fair prices, quick and Pan-India delivery.
A COMPANY, registered under The Indian Companies Act, and audited annually for financial and social compliances within the Company Law applicable in India. Promoted by professionals turned entrepreneurs who continue to have “hands on” roles. A team of highly motivated professionals who value opportunities and deliver excellence. An attitude of continuous improvement. Unmatched creativity.
